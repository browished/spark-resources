The Steps We Take

All files are properly scanned and tested before sharing to the public. Some of the tools we use include Kaspersky, MalwareBytes, VirusTotal, and Triage. All software is installed on our personal devices to ensure funtionality. We only take directly from reliable sources, this includes but is not limited to Team V.R, Monter Group, and Monkrus.These groups have distrubuted and developed repacks to the piracy community for years and are very well trusted. A handfew of the patches on our site were created by members of our staff team along with and a few outside supporters. Only a limited amount of inviduals have access to updating our website.

False Positives

False positives are not uncommon when pirating, especially when it comes to Windows Defender, Norton and McAfee. The majority of these files have been modified in order to get around licensing, and sometimes this behavior may be targeted. Any sort of AI, PUP (Potentially unwanted program), Machine Learning (!ML), Rep/Reputation and hacktool/crack detections can safely be dismissed.

Self Initiative

Google is free. Take initiative and research any concerns you might have. Scan the files with a reputable anti-virus, or better yet, run it in a virtual machine.

Ultimately, no one here is forcing you to use our website nor will we beg you to believe us, we started this for fun, remember..? Pirating will always come with its share of risk, and it's your responsibility to be smart about it. If you're unable to conclude your own research, you might be better off paying for your software.
